# ThoughtStream — Design System

A calm, earthy design system for journaling and mindfulness apps. Warm stone tones, generous whitespace, and understated interactions create a reflective, unhurried experience.

## Color Palette

- **Primary** (#57534E): Deep warm stone for primary actions and active elements.
- **Secondary** (#78716C): Muted stone for secondary actions and supporting UI.
- **Background** (#FAFAF9): Warm off-white page background.
- **Surface** (#FFFFFF): Clean white for cards and elevated surfaces.
- **Text Primary** (#1C1917): Near-black warm text for headings and body.
- **Text Secondary** (#78716C): Muted text for descriptions and metadata.
- **Text Placeholder** (#A8A29E): Light stone for placeholder text.
- **Border** (#D6D3D1): Warm stone border for inputs, cards, and dividers.
- **Focus** (#D946EF): Fuchsia focus ring for interactive elements.

## Typography

- **Display**: Inter 36px bold, 1.15 line height. Hero headlines.
- **Heading**: Inter 24px semi-bold, 1.25 line height. Section titles.
- **Body**: Inter 16px regular, 1.5 line height. Body text.
- **Caption**: Inter 12px medium, 1.3 line height. Labels and metadata.
